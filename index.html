I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at UC Merced, researching human-computer interaction and assistive technology in the Inclusive Interaction Lab. I specialize in developing haptic feedback systems for accessible navigation.

My work bridges user research, interaction design, and technology development to create inclusive solutions. I am a mixed methods researcher conducting usability studies, build prototypes, and apply machine learning to enhance user experiences, all focused on developing technology that meaningfully improves people's lives.

My research interests center on Human Factors, UX Research, Accessibility, and Intelligent Systems.

PROJECTS

TactileAlign: An Assistive HCI System for Visually Impaired Camera Alignment

A haptic system that guides visually impaired users to align mobile cameras using real-time haptic feedback through vibration motors.

Madhubani AI: Cultural Heritage Preservation via PEFT

Fine-tuning generative models to synthesize 2,500-year-old traditional Indian art, achieving authentic geometric precision where base models fail.

Multi-modal Ingredient Analyzer: AI-Driven Consumer Empowerment

An intelligent AI agent that reduces product research time by 95%, transforming complex ingredient lists into clear, fact-checked health insights in under 5 seconds.

TeachU: AI Learning Platform

An AI-powered platform that transforms static PDFs into animated educational videos in under a minute, presented at the 2024 TED AI Hackathon.

EchoFilter: Precision Spectrogram Optimization for Bird Calls

A dual-stage framework that isolates bird calls from environmental noise by combining raw audio denoising with targeted spectrogram enhancement to preserve key biological motifs.

Health Bot: Voice-Based Medical Assistant

A voice-activated diagnostic assistant that uses speech-to-text interaction and contextual memory to identify health issues with 95% precision.

Personalized Movie Recommender Systems

Two distinct recommender systems built in R using content-based and collaborative filtering to deliver personalized movie suggestions based on genre similarity and historical user preferences.
